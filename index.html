Privacy Policy for Guess the Synonym

Last updated: [Thursday, November 27th, 2025]

This Privacy Policy describes how Guess The Synonym ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Directly by Our App

Our App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game Statistics: Total games played, correct answers, points, streaks, and game performance data
  • Game Progress: Coins balance, hints used, time extensions used
  • App Preferences: Sound settings, music preferences, and other in-app settings

Important: This information is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

1.2 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Our App uses third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our App. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version, device identifiers)
  • Advertising ID (used for ad personalization)
  • Approximate location data (general location for ad targeting)
  • App usage data (interactions with ads, ad views)
  • IP address

For more information about how Google uses data, visit: Google Privacy Policy

Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)

When you make in-app purchases, Google Play processes the transaction and may collect:

  • Purchase history
  • Payment information (handled securely by Google)
  • Account information associated with your Google Play account

We do not have access to your payment details. All transactions are processed by Google Play.

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Local Game Data

Game statistics and preferences stored on your device are used solely to:

  • Maintain your game progress and statistics
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide personalized gameplay experience

2.2 Third-Party Data Usage

Third-party services use collected data to:

  • AdMob: Display relevant advertisements, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud
  • Google Play Billing: Process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However:

  • We use third-party services (AdMob, Google Play) that collect data as described above
  • These services have their own privacy policies governing data collection and use
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All game data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.
  • Third-Party Storage: Data collected by AdMob and Google Play is stored on their servers according to their privacy policies.
  • Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID)
  • Reset Game Data: You can reset your game statistics and data through the App's Settings menu
  • Manage In-App Purchases: You can manage or cancel in-app purchases through your Google Play account settings
  • Delete Local Data: Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored game data
  • Access Your Data: Contact us if you wish to know what data we have about you
